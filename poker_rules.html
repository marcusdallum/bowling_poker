Bowling Poker

The Sacred Chronicles of the Lanes

The Setup

Two decks, infused with the presence of Jokers, are bound together at the start of every trial. Each participant contributes one silver coin ($1) to the shared treasury. The player holding the cards acts as the Pot Custodian, guarding the treasury for the group.

The Trials of Skill

As the pins fall, cards are earned through prowess:

  • • A Strike: 1 Card
  • • A Consecutive Strike: 2 Cards
  • • A Spare: 1 Card
  • • The Split-to-Spare: 2 Cards

The Unaligned Observers

Up to two non-bowlers may join the fray. By selecting from the Ace, 2, 3, or 4, they adopt the destiny and card-drawing path of the bowler assigned to their chosen mark.

The Arcane Wild

Upon the final bowler's turn, the deck is cut. The revealed card becomes the Universal True Wild. It possesses the power to mimic any rank or any suit to complete a hand.

The Law of Purity (Natural Rule):

A hand of pure essence (no Wilds or Jokers) shall always triumph over an identical hand that is touched by the Wild or the Joker.

Victory & Destiny

To claim the treasury, one must possess both the Superior Hand and a Greater Score than their opponents. However, during the Season Finale, the requirement of the score is lifted; only the hand matters.
